CARDIOVASCULAR AND CEREBROVASCULAR
Blood Viscosity
  Meaning: Measures thickness of blood
  High: Risk of clotting
  Low: Risk of anemia
  Advice High: Drink water, consult doctor
  Advice Low: Iron rich food, supplements
Blood Fat
  Meaning: Cholesterol & triglycerides level
  High: Risk of heart disease
  Low: Risk of energy deficiency
  Advice High: Reduce fats, exercise
  Advice Low: Balanced diet, healthy fats

KIDNEY
Creatinine
  Meaning: Kidney function indicator
  High: Kidney stress
  Low: Rarely significant
  Advice High: Hydrate, consult doctor
